A friendly and open English speaking playgroup.

For children from 0-4yrs and their parents/ caregivers to meet, play and chat.

<
## Demo content We meet Wednesday mornings from 9:15am to 11:15am in the lower hall at St Ursula’s - Jubiläumsplatz 2, 3005 Bern. The cost is CHF 5 which includes a snack for the children and hot drinks and biscuits for the parents. You can just drop by unanounced we look forward meeting you! The group is run by parents on a voluntary basis - we all get involved. Join us on Facebook and Instagramm for the latest tattle and connect to our lively community of english speaking parents from and around Bern.
